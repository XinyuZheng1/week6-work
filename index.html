There are broad sense and narrow sense. Jewelry in the narrow sense only refers to jade products. Jewelry in the broad sense should include jewelry, handicrafts or other treasures made of gold, silver and natural materials (minerals, rocks, organisms, etc.) with certain value, which are collectively referred to as jewelry.

With the development of society and economy, in addition to natural gemstones and artificial gemstones, the concept of jewelry should be expanded to include gold, silver, jewelry, etc. These items are collectively referred to as the "jewelry industry". The scope of "jewelry" is much larger than the broad concept of "gem". Gemstones in a broad sense refer to those raw materials suitable for cutting or carving into jewelry or handicrafts.
